Himalayan Mountain Pass Tour

5 Days / 4 Nights 480 km Total Max 8 Riders 5,300m Altitude
5.0 (48 reviews)

Conquer the highest motorable roads in the world on this epic 5-day guided motorcycle tour through the Himalayas. Ride through the legendary Leh-Manali highway, cross five mountain passes above 4,000 meters, and experience breathtaking valley views, ancient monasteries, and rugged terrain that will test your skills and reward your spirit.

Our experienced guides have led hundreds of riders through these routes. Every detail is taken care of—from your bike and safety gear to comfortable overnight stays and warm meals at altitude. A support vehicle follows the group for complete peace of mind.

Tour Itinerary

D1

Arrival & Briefing

Arrive at base camp, meet your guide, bike handover, safety briefing, and gear fitting. Welcome dinner.

D2

Base Camp → Keylong (120 km)

Cross the Rohtang Pass at 3,978m. Stunning views of glaciers and alpine meadows. Overnight at Keylong.

D3

Keylong → Sarchu (110 km)

Ride through Baralacha La (4,890m). The most challenging and rewarding day with river crossings and high-altitude plains.

D4

Sarchu → Leh (130 km)

Cross Tanglang La (5,328m). Descend into the moonscape of Ladakh. Arrive at Leh for celebration dinner.

D5

Departure Day

Optional monastery visit, drone footage review, photo sharing, and farewell. Transfer to airport.
