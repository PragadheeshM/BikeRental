OUR STORY

From a Passion Project to a Global Riding Community

Founded in 2018 by a group of avid motorcycle enthusiasts, RideOn started with a simple idea: make premium motorcycle experiences accessible to everyone. What began as a small garage with five bikes has grown into a fleet of 500+ vehicles across multiple cities.

Today, we don't just rent bikes—we craft unforgettable journeys. Our expert guides, meticulously maintained fleet, and commitment to safety have made us the trusted choice for over 12,000 riders worldwide.

Our Mission

To make premium motorcycle experiences accessible to everyone, fostering a global community of riders who explore the world on two wheels with confidence and joy.

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted motorcycle rental and tour platform, connecting riders with the most breathtaking roads, cultures, and experiences on every continent.

Our Values

Safety first, customer obsession, sustainability, and passion. Every decision we make is guided by these core principles that define who we are as a company.

OUR COMMITMENT

Sustainable Travel & Community Impact

At RideOn, we believe that exploring the world should never come at the cost of its future. We are dedicated to reducing our environmental footprint while maximizing the positive impact we have on the local communities we visit.

Transitioning to Electric

By 2030, we aim to have 60% of our fleet composed of electric motorcycles and scooters, significantly reducing urban noise and emissions.

Supporting Local Guides

We partner exclusively with local guides and businesses in every tour destination, ensuring that the economic benefits of tourism stay within the community.

Safety Education

We provide free safety workshops and riding education to local communities, promoting responsible and safe riding habits globally.
